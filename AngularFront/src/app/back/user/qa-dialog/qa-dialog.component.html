What is Two-Factor Authentication (TFA)?

Two-Factor Authentication adds an extra layer of security to your account by requiring not only your password, but also an additional code that is sent to your mobile device.

How to enable TFA?

  1. Go to your profile settings.
  2. Click on the "Two Factor Authentication" tab.
  3. Click on the "Generate QR Code" button.
  4. Scan the QR code with your mobile device using a TFA app like Google Authenticator.
  5. Enter the verification code provided by the app.
  6. Click on the "Enable Two-Factor Authentication" button.
